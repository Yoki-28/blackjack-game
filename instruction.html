Blackjack Game Instructions

  1. Objective: Get the sum of your cards as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it. If your total exceeds 21, you lose.
  2. How to Play:
    • Click the "START GAME" button to begin.
    • Two random cards will be dealt to you.
    • Check the card sum displayed on the screen.
    • If the sum is less than 21, you can draw another card by clicking the "NEW CARD" button.
  3. Winning Conditions:
    • If your card sum equals 21, you win with a Blackjack! ðŸŽ‰
    • The game will reset for a new round.
  4. Losing Conditions:
    • If your card sum exceeds 21, you lose.
    • The game will reset for a new round.
  5. Continue Playing: Decide whether to draw a new card or stop playing if your sum is below 21.
  6. Tips:
    • Aces (11) are powerful but risky if your total is close to 21.
    • Think carefully before drawing a new card when your sum is close to 21.

Enjoy the game, and may luck be on your side!
